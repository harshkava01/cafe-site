Our Story

Founded in 2000, La Fiorito - The Urban Cafe has been serving the community with passion and dedication. Our mission is to create a space where exceptional coffee meets genuine hospitality, making every visit a memorable experience.

The café had been in the town for as long as anyone could remember, and it had become a beloved institution among the locals. The café was run by a friendly and hospitable couple, Mr. and Mrs. Oberois.

EcLipse EspRessO this is our first branch in India located in Mumbai.
